EID ul Adha

Eid ul-Adha, is a significant Islamic celebration marking the willingness of Prophet Ibrahim to sacrifice his son in obedience to God. Celebrated with communal prayers, feasting, and the sacrifice of animals, it emphasizes charity and sharing with the less fortunate. The festival fosters unity and spiritual reflection among Muslims worldwide.
